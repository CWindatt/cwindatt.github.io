École Noël Ritchot Dojang:


École Noël Ritchot (Gymnasium)
45, de la Digue Ave.
Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada

Classes are taught in the gymnasium of Ecole Noel-Ritchot. It is a spacious facility with mats, change-rooms and lots of parking. There is also a soccer and baseball field just outside, providing a great space for outdoor training. It is minutes away from most South end communities and only 15 minutes from downtown.

The school is beside Pembina Hwy and the Farmers Market, just south of the Perimeter. Enter through the gymnasium doors beside the parking lot. If you are heading South on Pembina, pull a u-turn just after the Farmer's market (at Lemay Ave. and "The Nob" restaurant/bar), then pull an immediate right (onto Avenue De La Digue) and the school will be in front of you.
