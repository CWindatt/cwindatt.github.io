LaBelle Taekwondo offers classes for all ages and skill levels. If you are at all interested, come down and try out the class for free!

Classes are separated into a children's class and adult class. High belts from the children's class are invited to participate in the adult class, while high belts from the adult class will often act as assistant instructors for the children's class.
