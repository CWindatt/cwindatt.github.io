What is Taekwondo?

TaeKwonDo is a Korean martial art of self defense. We teach TaeKwonDo in �old school� style, meaning that your training will be tradi- tional and physically demanding. Our school is about learning and preserving the techniques and principles behind TaeKwonDo. Students are expected to work hard. In return, they receive a rewarding and satisfying eduction.

Can Taekwondo be used for self defense?

Students are taught practical self defense techniques and learn strategy to spot dangers and avoid dangerous places, people and activities. Common sense techniques are taught to get out of dangerous situations.

Why take Taekwondo?

TaeKwonDo provides students with many benefits in addition to martial arts and self defense skills. Students learn self confidence, respect, good manners, improve fitness (speed, strength, flexibility and endurance) and also provides motivation, discipline, coordination and body balance. These skills as well as overall physical fitness are part of our TaeKwonDo training.
